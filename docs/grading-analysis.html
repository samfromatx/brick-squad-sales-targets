Grading Decision Framework
Run every card through this before submitting.
Step 1 → Is PSA 9 profitable? (at $35 cost)
    YES → Safe to grade (Tier 1). Submit.
    NO → Continue to Step 2.
 
Step 2 → Is gem rate > 50%?
    YES → Grade if EV > $75 and card is clean.
    NO → Continue to Step 3.
 
Step 3 → Is PSA 10 profit > 5× the PSA 9 loss?
    YES → Grade only cherry-picked copies.
    NO → Don't grade.
